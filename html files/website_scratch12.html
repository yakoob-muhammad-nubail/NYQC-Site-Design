About Us

The Nazeer Yakoob Quality and Compliance Inc

The NYQC is a Management System Consulting and Training Services company. Based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. The organization provides Management System services to a broad range of organizations, both service and manufacturing.

  • Our principals have extensive experience in executive management, third party auditing and management system development.
  • Our philosophy is to form partnerships with our clients that will provide mutual benefit and success through full client engagement.
  • Our belief is that management systems should be unique to a company and be structured to yield continual business improvement and improved customer satisfaction.
  • Our approach ensures that the corporate direction and objectives are understood and that the management system is designed to facilitate these goals.
  • Our methods of service ensures the transfer of knowledge and management system ownership to our client.
